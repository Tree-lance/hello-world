Landlords

There are many reasons why you own your property. It may be that your an investor or a first time landlord or it may just be that you own a property through it being passed down you. If you are any of the above we can help.

At Guaranteed Rent 2 Rent, we ensure that your mortgage or rent is paid each and every month. We also ensure that your property is maintained on a montlhy basis hopefully aliviating any stress or concerns you may have when owning a property.

How it works

1. We let you know how much Guaranteed Rent we can offer you.

Prior to any decision made, we like to meet with you to discuss what we can offer and provide an explanation as to how the process works.

2. We refurbish your property (if necessary)

Should your property require refurbishment, we complete minor works to ensure your property is let out to a high standard. Should major works be required we will discuss this at the initial meeting.

3. We let your property out to professional tenants

We give you the total freedom from having to manage tenants within your prpoerty. We find the tenants, we let each room out individually and maintain your property on a monthly basis

4. We pay you a Guaranteed Rent for a fixed period.

We do not charge you any management fees and the utility bills are covered by us. We simply pay you the agreed rent every month. We take the risk away from you, as if the rooms are empty or utilities bills are high you get paid and we don't.

5. You sit back, relax and focus on other things.

You receive no more calls at evenings or weekends. We will be the point of contact for tenants within your property. We will manage your property and cover minor works ourselves.
